Vishwabharti Mahavidhyalaya, Cidco, Nanded has been operational for the last twelve years. We have been trying to provide quality education along with an emphasis on the improvement of communication cleverness and training in relevant fields. We know that quality is a defining element of education in today’s world. To improve the quality of education additional, we have decided to follow the SRTM university education criteria for performance excellence in Higher Education along with ISO norms of Quality Management System. We aim to redefine education in terms of the imperatives of the 21st century in the triple context of Quality, morals and responsibility.
